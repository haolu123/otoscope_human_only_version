Otoscope Exam Instructions

1. Open otoscope_exam.exe.

2. Enter your name in the Name field, then click Start Exam.

3. If you have taken this exam before with the same name, you may be asked to choose:
   - Continue Selected Test: continue a previous test
   - Start New Test: begin a new test
   - Cancel: return without starting

4. For each question, watch the otoscope video and choose one diagnosis from the options below the video:
   - AOM
   - Effusion
   - Normal
   - Perforation
   - Retraction
   - Tubes
   - Tympanosclerosis

5. Video controls:
   - Play: start or continue the video
   - Pause: pause the video
   - Replay: restart the video
   - 0.5x, 1x, 1.5x, 2x: change playback speed
   - Download Video: save a copy of the current video to a location you choose

6. After selecting an answer, click Confirm.

7. You can use:
   - Back: return to a previous question
   - Next: move forward to a question you have already visited
   - Go to New Question: jump back to the next unanswered question

8. You may change an earlier answer by clicking Back, selecting a different answer, and clicking Confirm again.

9. Your progress is saved automatically. If you close the program before finishing, you can reopen it, enter the same name, and continue a previous test.

10. When all questions are completed, the program will save your results automatically.
